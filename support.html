Frequently Asked Questions

Why can't I hear anything?

Check these:

  • Is the play button pressed? (should show pause icon)
  • Is the volume turned up in the app?
  • Is the speaker icon showing as muted?
  • Is your device volume turned up?
  • Is your device in silent mode? (background audio still works)

Does it use a lot of battery?

hushhh is optimized for minimal battery usage. Playing audio continuously will use some battery, but using the snooze timer to auto-stop after you fall asleep helps conserve power.

Does it work offline?

Yes! All sounds are built into the app. No internet connection required.

Do you collect my data?

No. We collect zero personal information. See our Privacy Policy for details.

Can I use it with other apps?

Yes! hushhh plays in the background while you use other apps. Perfect for focus while working or browsing.
