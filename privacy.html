Overview

hushhh ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your information.

Information Collection

We collect ZERO personal information.

hushhh does not:

  • Collect any personal data
  • Track your usage
  • Store any information about you
  • Require accounts or sign-ins
  • Use analytics or tracking services
  • Share data with third parties
  • Display advertisements

Data Storage

All app settings (volume level, selected sound, timer settings) are stored locally on your device only. This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is not backed up to our servers (we don't have servers)
  • Is deleted if you uninstall the app
  • Cannot be accessed by us or anyone else

Audio Playback

The white noise audio files are:

  • Bundled within the app
  • Played locally on your device
  • Not streamed from the internet
  • Not recorded or monitored

Permissions

The app requires only these system permissions:

  • Audio Playback: To play white noise sounds
  • Background Audio: To continue playing when screen is locked or when using other apps

We do not request access to:

  • Camera
  • Photos
  • Location
  • Contacts
  • Microphone (we only play audio, never record)
  • Files
  • Health data
  • Any other device features

Third-Party Services

hushhh does not integrate with any third-party services, SDKs, or analytics platforms.

Children's Privacy

hushhh is safe for all ages (rated 4+). Since we collect no data whatsoever, there is no information collected from children or anyone else.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever change this privacy policy, we will update this document and the "Last Updated" date. However, our commitment to collecting zero data will not change.
